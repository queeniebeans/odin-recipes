Baked Chicken Nuggets

Description

Chicken meat dipped in egg wash, rolled in panko bread crumbs and seasoned with paprika and spices, baked into crispy bites!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Panko Crumbs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Egg
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450F.
  2. Cut and prepare the chicken thighs, set aside.
  3. Prepare the egg wash in a shallow dish.
  4. Mix panko bread crumbs and seasoning in separate bowl until well mixed, set aside.
  5. Prepare a wire rack over a baking sheet lined with aluminum foil and spray with non-stick spray.
  6. Using tongs or chopsticks, take each piece of chicken and submerge in the egg wash. Then, roll in the panko bread mix until covered.
  7. Place on oiled wire rack about 1in apart from each other. Repeat until all the chicken is dipped and breaded.
  8. Bake until golden brown and cooked well on the inside. Enjoy hot with dipping sauce of choice!
