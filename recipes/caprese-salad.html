Caprese Salad

Description

A savoury and classy salad combining the umami profile of tomatos with the creamy richness of mozzarella, perfectly accented by basil leaves and a balsamic vinegar and honey reduction.

Ingredients

  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil leaves
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Cook balsamic vinegar and honey together in a small saucepan over medium heat. Once it thickens, set aside to cool.
  2. Cut quarter inch thick slices of the tomato and mozzarella.
  3. On a flat dish, start laying down slices of tomato, mozzarella, and basil leaves in alternating order.
  4. Once arranged, drizzle over top with the cooled balsamic vinegar and honey reduction.
  5. Serve on its own or with crackers!
